Magento
Enterprise Cloud Edition

Faster, Better, Less Hassle

GET STARTED

Let Magento Handle It

Stop worrying about monitoring your servers. Stop worrying about upgrades and security. With Magento Enterprise Cloud Edition we take care of everything. Now you can deploy faster, with a managed service and cloud hosting that is optimized for the Magento platform. It lets you focus on your business. Because you shouldn’t fear traffic spikes and the holidays, you should celebrate them.

Keep it Simple

By hosting your store in the cloud, you can ensure your content is delivered fast, and your site is optimized at all times, no matter where your customer is. You also get real time performance analytics to make sure your site is working hard, 24/7.

Move Fast

Choose the platform that can change at the speed of commerce. Magento Enterprise Cloud Edition provides the development technologies and tools to streamline development processes, and get your new content live, faster. You can experiment and innovate with staging and previewing capabilities. Because everything is faster in the cloud.

Commerce Confidence

Black Friday sales spikes? We’ve got you covered. Magento Enterprise Cloud Edition is built on AWS, the world’s leading cloud environment. This highly available environment is designed to scale and grow with your success. It’ll fire up when sales and traffic get heavy, and ensure your site won’t go down at the wrong time.

Best-in-Class Technologies

Magento Enterprise Cloud Edition brings together industry-leading technologies at all levels of the stack–AWS for hosting, platform.sh for platform management, New Relic and Blackfire.io for performance management tools, and Fastly for CDN. The Magento Enterprise Edition 2.1 codebase offers limitless flexibility, giving your business the competitive edge.
